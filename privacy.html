Privacy Policy

Welcome to Kishanquarks, an online gaming platform where users can play games, participate in contests, and win rewards and prizes.

Your privacy is extremely important to us, and we are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect the minimum information required to run your account and allow you to participate in games.

We may collect:

  • Name (optional)
  • Email address (used for login)
  • Phone number (optional, for account recovery)
  • Basic account details (to check whether you are a Prime member or a normal user)
  • Game activity (scores, winnings, participation)

We DO NOT collect:

  • Aadhar card
  • PAN card
  • Bank details
  • Credit/debit card details
  • Any other sensitive personal information

You only pay ₹1 entry fee, and the payment is handled by a trusted payment gateway, not by us directly.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used only to provide our services, nothing else. We use your data for:

  • Logging you into your account
  • Verifying your membership level (Prime or normal user)
  • Allowing you to play games
  • Reward distribution
  • Preventing cheating or multiple fake accounts
  • Improving user experience

We DO NOT sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third-party for marketing.

3. No Data Selling

We strictly do not sell your data to anyone.

Your data stays safely within our system and is used only to:

  • Identify your account
  • Verify your game entry
  • Maintain fairness in the platform

4. User Responsibilities

To keep the platform fair, users must follow these rules:

  • Do not create multiple accounts
  • Do not use fake information
  • Do not try to cheat or exploit any game
  • Do not use bots or scripts
  • Follow all platform rules and instructions

Warning: If a user breaks these rules, their account may be suspended or banned permanently without refund.

5. Payments

You pay ₹1 participation fee to enter the games.

All payments are processed through secure third-party payment gateways. We never store your card or bank details.

6. Cookies & Usage Data

We may use cookies to:

  • Keep you logged in
  • Improve loading speed
  • Save your game preferences

You can turn off cookies from your browser, but the website may not work properly.

7. Data Security

We use industry-standard security practices to protect your data:

  • Encrypted servers
  • Protected login system
  • Secure payment integration

Although no online platform is 100% hack-proof, we take every step to keep your data safe.

8. Children’s Privacy

Our service is not intended for users under 13 years old.

If we find any account made by a child, it will be removed.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally.

If we make major changes, we will notify users on our website.
